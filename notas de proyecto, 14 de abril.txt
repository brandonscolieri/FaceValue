Notes about project so far:

1) AffectNet: both categorial and dimensional models covered
  a) Eleven categories that images can fall under:
    * Seven facial emotions
    * Neutral, None, Uncertain, and Non-face categories
  b) Valence and Arousal Annotation
    * x, y, axes are valence and arousal, respectively
2) Over 1 million images
  a) Information for the images are located in CSV files
    * Includes info such as file path, face location, etc.
    * Expression category, valence, and arousal labeled
  b) In their paper, they cropped and resized to 256 * 256
